Contributions to the iSphere Project

We are open to developers who want to contribute their ideas and manpower to the iSphere project. If you are good in RPG and you know how to write Eclipse plug-ins, you are invited to join the iSphere project team.

We regret that we do not have the time teach you how to write Eclipse plug-ins. Our daily work (we have to earn a living), our families, and the iSphere project fill up all the available time. We hope you understand.

If you want to join us, please drop us an email and tell us about your ideas and experience.
