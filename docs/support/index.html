Getting Support

When you are in doubt whether or not something is wrong with iSphere, please, start with the iSphere help and check for remarks regarding your concerns or ask your questions at the WDSCI-L mailing list. In case you think that you spotted a bug, add a bug report on the iSphere Issues page.

Start with searching the help pages.
Ask your questions at the WDSCI-L mailing list at midrange.com.
For bugs open a ticket at the iSphere Issues page.
